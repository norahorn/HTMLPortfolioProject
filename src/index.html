Who I am

I’m a UX researcher and designer, holding a Master of Science degree from Copenhagen Business School. I have a great passion for ideas and projects in the rich interplay of technological trends, services and innovations.

Diving into new fields is something that I love, which means I can take on projects out of my comfort zone. Most recently, I was involved in a field trip to Mumbai, where I helped a start-up to test and iterate their prototype and to gain a better understanding of their target group.

In my free time, I like challenging my fear of heights by climbing and bouldering and exploring the live music scene in Copenhagen. I volunteer at the cultural center Huset KBH.

What I do

I have a throrough understanding of user-centered design and research methods. Ich beherrsche prototyping tools wie Axure, Adobe XD und Balsamiq. I am comfortable to do both, low-fi and high-fi prototypes. I love going on paper!

At the moment, I am teaching myself how to code. I am taking classes and assignments on codeschool.com for HTML CSS and Javascript. Every single line of code for this portfolio has been written by me! Learning from scratch.
